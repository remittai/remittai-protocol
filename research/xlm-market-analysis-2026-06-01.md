## Relevance to Token Launch Strategy

### Why XLM/Stellar Matters for Our Project

Stellar is significant not just as a trading asset but as a potential **launch chain** or **integration partner** for a token project:

1. **Built for payments & asset issuance** — Stellar's core purpose is issuing custom tokens and facilitating cross-border payments. Launching a token on Stellar is faster and cheaper than Ethereum.

2. **Institutional connections already exist** — Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, Circle (USDC) are already integrated. A token on Stellar inherits proximity to these rails.

3. **Soroban smart contracts** — Stellar's smart contract platform (Soroban) launched in 2024, enabling DeFi and more complex token mechanics on a payments-optimized chain.

4. **Lower competition** — Solana and Base are crowded with meme tokens and AI agent plays. Stellar has fewer token launches, meaning less noise and more visibility.

5. **Regulatory positioning** — Stellar Development Foundation has been compliance-forward, working with regulated entities. Better positioning for RWA or institutional-facing tokens.

### Potential Play: Stellar-Based Token

| Advantage | Detail |
|---|---|
| Transaction cost | ~0.00001 XLM per tx (fractions of a cent) |
| Finality | 3-5 second settlement |
| Built-in DEX | Stellar has a native order book — no need for Uniswap-style AMM |
| Anchor system | Fiat on/off ramps built into the protocol |
| Compliance tools | Built-in asset controls (authorization, clawback for regulated assets) |

### Risk Factors
- Smaller DeFi ecosystem than Solana/Base
- Less retail hype / meme culture (harder for community tokens)
- Developer tooling less mature than EVM chains
- XLM price action still unproven at macro level
