# 07 — Go-to-Market Strategy

## GTM Philosophy

**"Crypto is the engine. The user never sees it."**

RemittAI is NOT marketed as a crypto project to end users. It's marketed as the cheapest, fastest way to send money home. The token and blockchain are invisible infrastructure — like how Visa users don't think about SWIFT.

**Two audiences, two strategies:**
1. **Crypto community** — Token investors, liquidity providers, AI agent developers (knows about blockchain)
2. **Remittance users** — Diaspora communities, migrant workers, families (doesn't know or care about blockchain)

### Messaging (Non-Crypto)

**DO say:**
- "Send money home for under 1% — no hidden fees"
- "Your family gets paid in 5 seconds, straight to their phone"
- "No bank account needed — pay out to M-Pesa, GCash, or cash pickup"
- "See exactly what they'll receive before you send"

**DON'T say:**
- "Blockchain-powered remittances"
- "Decentralized finance for the unbanked"
- "Buy $RMTAI tokens"
- Any crypto jargon

### Channel Strategy (Remittance Users)

| Channel | Tactic | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Facebook Groups** | Filipino, Mexican, Nigerian diaspora community groups | Where these communities already gather |
| **WhatsApp** | Referral sharing, "send $5 free" campaigns | Primary communication tool for target users |
| **TikTok** | "I saved $XX sending money with [app]" testimonial videos | Viral potential, young diaspora audience |
| **Community events** | Filipino festivals, Mexican cultural events in US cities | In-person trust building |
| **Church/mosque partnerships** | Trusted community institutions | Credibility in target demographics |
| **Remittance comparison sites** | List on SaveOnSend, Monito, RemitFinder | Capture intent-based traffic |

### Referral Program

| Action | Reward |
|---|---|
| Refer a friend who completes first send | Sender gets $5 credit, friend gets free first transfer |
| Referred friend sends 3+ times | Original referrer gets additional $5 |
| Top referrers (monthly) | Bonus $RMTAI airdrop (crypto-savvy users) or cash credit (mainstream users) |

**Target:** Each user refers 2-3 friends. Organic viral loop in tight-knit diaspora communities.
