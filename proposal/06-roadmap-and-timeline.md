## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Anchor partners fail/exit | No fiat off-ramp in corridor | Multi-anchor strategy per corridor; build own anchor capability |
| Regulatory crackdown | Token classified as security | Legal opinion letter; utility-first design; exclude US if needed |
| Low liquidity on DEX | Poor FX rates, bad UX | Staking incentives; liquidity mining; market maker partnerships |
| Competitor launches first | Market share loss | AI differentiation; mobile money focus; underserved corridor strategy |
| Smart contract exploit | Loss of funds, trust | Multiple audits; bug bounty program; gradual TVL scaling |
| XLM price crash | Higher real cost for Stellar operations | Minimal XLM dependency; USDC-denominated operations |
| Team departure | Development stalls | Open-source codebase; DAO transition; vested tokens require continued contribution |
| Remittix captures market | Reduced investor interest | Different chain (Stellar vs ETH), different market (unbanked vs crypto holders), AI moat |
