## #announcements — Pinned Welcome Post

---

Hey everyone, welcome to RemittAI.

I started this project because something about remittances has always bothered me. People working abroad, sending money back to their families — and getting hit with 6-10% fees every single time. That's rent money. That's groceries. That's a kid's school supplies. And it just disappears into Western Union's pocket.

We're building something different.

RemittAI is an AI-powered remittance protocol on Stellar. The short version:
- Send money home for under 1%. Not 6%. Not 5%. Under 1%.
- It arrives in 5 seconds. Not 3 days.
- No bank account needed — pay out to mobile money, cash pickup, whatever works
- AI finds the cheapest route for every single transfer. Every time.

The tech is already live on Stellar testnet. Token is issued. Smart contracts are written. AI routing engine is working. This isn't a whitepaper project — we're building in the open and you can watch every step.

What's coming:
- Presale in the next few months
- First live corridor (US to Philippines or Mexico)
- Soroban contracts getting audited through SDF's Audit Bank
- Mobile app in beta

This Discord is where it all happens. Dev updates, governance discussions, testnet feedback, and just hanging out with people who think the remittance industry is broken and want to fix it.

A few things about how we run this community:
- We build in public. You'll see wins and you'll see problems. That's the deal.
- Your feedback actually matters. We're going to let token holders vote on which corridors we expand to next.
- No fake hype. We're not going to shill you with rocket emojis and "1000x incoming" posts. The product speaks for itself.
- Ask anything. Seriously. If I can't answer it, I'll say so.

Jump into #general-chat and say hey. Tell us where you're from and what brought you here. If you've ever sent money abroad or received remittances, I especially want to hear your story — you're the reason this project exists.

Let's build.

— cps123, Founder

## #rules — Server Rules

---

Welcome to RemittAI. Keep it simple:

**1. Be respectful.** We're a global community. Treat everyone the way you'd want your family treated. No racism, sexism, harassment, or personal attacks. Zero tolerance.

**2. No scams or shilling.** Don't DM people offering "deals." Don't promote random tokens. Don't impersonate team members. RemittAI team will NEVER DM you first asking for money or private keys.

**3. Stay on topic.** Price talk goes in #price-discussion. Dev questions go in #dev-updates. Keep channels clean so people can find what they need.

**4. No financial advice.** Nothing said here is investment advice. We talk about what we're building, not what your portfolio should look like. Do your own research.

**5. English is the main language.** We'll add regional channels as the community grows (Filipino, Spanish, etc.). For now, keep the main channels in English so everyone can follow.

**6. Report issues.** See a scammer? Someone being shady? Tag @Mod or use the report function. We'll handle it.

Break the rules and you get one warning. Break them again and you're out.
